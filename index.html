How to Use:

  1. Choose the text you would like to copy to another device by placing it in the following text box, or simply copy the text.
  2. Send your text to Online Clipboard by clicking the " Send Data or Text" radio button and filling the text box, then submitting it.
  3. On the other device, retrieve your text from Online Clipboard by entering the provided code in the text box after clicking the "Receive Data or Text" radio button.
